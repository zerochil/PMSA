About the PMSA Discussion Board

The objectives of this discussion board are to give current undergraduate students who are active members of the PMSA the ability to ask postgraduate alumni any questions they may have in regards to Medical, Dental, Veterinarian, Physician Assistant, or Pharmaceutical Science graduate schools

Get Envolved!

  • Engage in career-related conversation between you and former PMSA graduates.
  • Give yourself an opportunity to gain knowledge about the field you are interested in.
  • Learn more about the applying, interviewing, and attending of graduate schools.
